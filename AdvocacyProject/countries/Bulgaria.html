Bulgaria

This Balkan nation has a unique climate that allows it to produce unique wines, cheeses and yogurt. It has many similarities to Russian, Italian, Greek, and even Middle Eastern cuisines.

Banitza

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 1/4 pound of feta cheese
  • 1/2 cup of cottage cheese
  • 2 tablespoon full of plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoon full of ricota cheese
  • 1 pack of fillo dough
  • 1/4 cup oil for fillo leaves only.

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. First mix eggs until combine then add feta cheese, cottage cheese, plain yogurt, and ricotta cheese; mix well until all ingredients are combined.
  3. Open fillo package and use one sheet of fillo at the time. Make sure you oil each sheet of fillo dough with oil and then fill with cheese and wrap and place in a pan with sides.
  4. Bake for 30 to 35 minutes until golden brown.
